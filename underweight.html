VEG DIET FOR BULKING


Breakfast: Paneer Paratha with Curd

Ingredients:

  • 2 whole wheat parathas stuffed with paneer (cottage cheese) filling (approx. 10g protein per paratha)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (6-8g protein)

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 20-26g
  • Total Calories: Around 400-500 calories

Mid-Morning Snack: Banana Shake with Almonds

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup milk or almond milk (8g protein)
  • Handful of almonds (6g protein)

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 14g
  • Total Calories: Around 300-350 calories

Lunch: Vegetable Pulao with Raita

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked vegetable pulao (made with brown rice for added nutrition) (approx. 6g protein)
  • 1 cup cucumber and tomato raita (4-6g protein)

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 10-12g
  • Total Calories: Around 400-500 calories

Afternoon Snack: Chickpea Chaat

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas (15g protein)
  • Chopped onions, tomatoes, and green chilies
  • Lemon juice and chaat masala for seasoning

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 15g
  • Total Calories: Around 250-300 calories

Dinner: Dal Fry with Roti

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dal fry (made with lentils like masoor dal or moong dal) (approx. 15g protein)
  • 2 whole wheat rotis (approx. 5g protein each)

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 25g
  • Total Calories: Around 400-500 calories

Evening Snack: Soy Milk with Peanut Butter Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 glass of soy milk (8g protein)
  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread with peanut butter (8-10g protein)

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 16-18g
  • Total Calories: Around 300-350 calories

Total Daily Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Approximately 100-105g
  • Total Calories: Around 2150-2500 calories
