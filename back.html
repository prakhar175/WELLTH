Back Exercises

Deadlifts

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, barbell over your feet. Bend at your hips and knees, keeping your back straight. Grab the bar with an overhand grip, lift to hip level, then return to the floor.

Lat Pulldown

Sit at a lat pulldown machine with knees secured under the pads. Grasp the bar with a wide overhand grip and pull it down to your chest. Slowly release the bar back up to the starting position.

Bent over rows

Hold a barbell with hands shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent. Lean forward from your hips, keeping your back straight. Pull the barbell towards your waist.

Pull Ups

Hang from a pull-up bar with palms facing forward. Pull yourself up until your chin passes the bar, then lower back down.Keep your legs straight during the motion.

T Bar Rows

Straddle a T-Bar row machine, knees slightly bent, and grab the handles. Keep your back straight, hinge at the hips, and pull the handles towards your chest.
