Abs Exercises

Crunches

Crunches target the rectus abdominis muscle. Lie on your back with knees bent, hands behind your head, and lift your shoulders off the floor while keeping your lower back pressed into the mat.

Plank

The plank is an isometric exercise that engages multiple core muscles. Hold a push-up position with your body forming a straight line from head to heels, engaging your core muscles.

Leg Raises

Leg raises primarily target the lower abs. Lie on your back with legs straight, lift your legs towards the ceiling, and then lower them back down without touching the floor.

Bicycle Crunches

Bicycle crunches engage both the rectus abdominis and obliques. Lie on your back, bring your knees towards your chest, and alternately twist your torso to touch your elbow to the opposite knee.

Russian Twist

The Russian twist targets the oblique muscles. Sit on the floor with knees bent, lean back slightly, and rotate your torso from side to side while holding a weight or keeping your hands clasped together.
