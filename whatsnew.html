The Real Challenge

Calisthenics

Pull ups

Grab the bar with an overhand grip, wider than shoulder-width apart. Pull up, bringing your chest to the bar and chin over it. Lower down slowly with control.

Jump Squats

Perform jump squats: squat down, then jump up explosively. Land softly, immediately squatting again. Maintain proper form: chest up, back straight, knees aligned with toes.

Chin-Ups

Hang from bar, underhand grip, hands shoulder-width. Pull up, chin over bar. Lower with control, engaging core. Maintain straight back.

Dips

Grab parallel bars, shoulder-width grip, elbows slightly bent. Lower until arms parallel to ground. Push back up, fully extend arms.

Crunches

Lie on back, knees bent, hands behind head. Lift shoulders, contract abs to bring chest toward knees.Focus on controling the reps.
