NON VEG DIET FOR BULKING


Breakfast: Scrambled Eggs with Whole Wheat Toast and Avocado

Ingredients:

  • 3 whole eggs (18g protein)
  • 2 slices of whole wheat toast (6g protein)
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 24g
  • Total Calories: Around 400 calories

Mid-Morning Snack:Greek Yogurt with Mixed Berries

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (20g protein)
  • Handful of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 20gm
  • Total Calories: Around 150 calories

Lunch:Grilled Chicken Breast with Quinoa and Steamed Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz grilled chicken breast (approx. 40g protein)
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa (4g protein)
  • Steamed broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 44g
  • Total Calories: Around 500 calories

Afternoon Snack:Protein Shake with Almond Milk and Banana

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop whey protein powder (20-25g protein)
  • 1 cup almond milk (2g protein)
  • 1 ripe banana

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 22-27g
  • Total Calories: Around 250 calories

Dinner: Salmon Fillet with Sweet Potato and Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz grilled or baked salmon fillet (approx. 30g protein)
  • 1 medium sweet potato, baked (4g protein)
  • Steamed asparagus spears

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 34g
  • Total Calories: Around 500 calories

Evening Snack:Cottage Cheese with Pineapple

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese (14g protein)
  • Fresh pineapple chunks

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 14g
  • Total Calories: Around 150g

Total Daily Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Approximately 154-159g
  • Total Calories: Around 1950-2050 calories
