Shoulder Exercises

Shoulder Press

The shoulder press primarily targets the deltoid muscles. It involves pressing weights overhead while standing or seated.

Lateral Raises

Lateral raises target the lateral deltoid head. Lift dumbbells or cables out to the sides while keeping the arms slightly bent at the elbows..

Front Raises

Front raises target the anterior deltoid head. Lift weights (dumbbells or cables) forward, typically to shoulder height, while keeping the arms straight or slightly bent.

Reverse Flies

Reverse flyes target the posterior deltoid head and the muscles of the upper back. Bend forward at the waist and lift the weights out to the sides.

Arnold Press

The Arnold press combines a shoulder press with a twisting motion. Start with dumbbells held at shoulder height with palms facing towards you, then press them overhead while rotating the palms to face forward.
