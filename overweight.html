VEG DIET FOR WEIGHT LOSS


Breakfast: Vegetable Oats Upma

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats (4g protein)
  • Assorted vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers) (approx. 2g protein)
  • Mustard seeds, curry leaves, and spices for seasoning

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 6gm
  • Total Calories: Around 250 calories

Mid-Morning Snack:Greek Yogurt with Mixed Berries

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (20g protein)
  • Handful of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 20gm
  • Total Calories: Around 150 calories

Lunch:Mixed Vegetable Salad with Grilled Tofu

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens like spinach
  • 100g grilled tofu (approx. 10g protein)
  • Sliced cucumber, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil and lemon dressing

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 10g
  • Total Calories: Around 300 calories

Afternoon Snack: Roasted Chickpeas

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas (15g protein)
  • Spices like cumin, paprika, and garlic powder for seasoning

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 15g
  • Total Calories: Around 200 calories

Dinner:Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa (4g protein) Mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, mushrooms)
  • Tossed in soy sauce and sesame oil

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 4g
  • Total Calories: Around 300 calories

Evening Snack: Mixed Nuts

Ingredients:

  • Handful of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews) (approx. 6-8g protein)

Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Around 6-8g
  • Total Calories: Around 200g

Total Daily Nutritional Info:

  • Total Protein: Approximately 53-55g
  • Total Calories: Around 1550-1700 calories
