Chest Exercises

Dumbell Flyes

Lie on a flat bench with a dumbbell in each hand. Extend your arms above your chest with palms facing each other. Lower the dumbbells in a wide arc until you feel a stretch in your chest.

Push Ups

Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor. Push yourself back up to the starting position.Keep your back straight while doing it.

Bench Press

Lie on a flat bench with a barbell at eye level. Grasp the barbell with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower the barbell to your chest, then push it back up to the starting position.

Cable Crossover

Set the cable pulleys to chest height. Stand between the pulleys and grab the handles. Step forward slightly and bring your hands together in front of you and Slowly return to the starting position.

Inclined Bench Press

Lie on an inclined bench with a barbell at eye level. Grasp the barbell with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower the barbell and then push it back up to the starting position.
