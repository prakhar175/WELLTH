Legs Exercises

Squats

Squats target the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending your knees and hips, keeping your chest up, and then return to standing position.

Lunges

Lunges work the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. Step forward with one leg, lower your body until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle, and then push back to starting position..

Deadlifts

Deadlifts primarily target the hamstrings and lower back. Stand with feet hip-width apart, hold a barbell or dumbbells in front of you, hinge at the hips, and lower the weight towards the ground while keeping your back straight.

Calf raises

Calf raises focus on the calf muscles. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, raise your heels off the ground as high as possible, and then lower them back down.

Leg press

The leg press targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. Sit on a leg press machine, press the platform away from you by extending your knees, and then return to starting position.
